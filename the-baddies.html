Flora McEvil

Don’t let her sunny face fool you- this is one dastardly lady! We’re not sure why, but Flora has been after the Dazzles on many occasions. She’s captured, snatched, hi-jacked and shanghaied many a Dazzle - and doing some of her dirtiest work from jail! Yes, she finally got locked up, and that’s where she’ll stay. We hope...

Carly McEvil

So THIS is how Flora was able to perpetrate evil deeds on the Dazzles from jail— she used her daughter Carly! These two blonde baddies really dislike the Dazzles. And while Flora may be the mastermind, Carly is the sneaky one. Dazzles, watch out - Carly is comin’ to getcha!
