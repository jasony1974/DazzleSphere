Too Many Puppies!

Chapter 1: The Camping Trip

“How deep into the woods are we going to go?” asks Josephine.
“Diamond and I scoped out a really great spot for the tents last week,” Primrose tells her.

“I do love this spot!” Diamond says as they set up their tent.
“I’m so happy with this camp site!” Primrose exclaims.

A perfect camping site!
“We’re gooood camp site makers,” says Glissen

The girls rejoice in their hard work and decide to build a campfire and tell spooky stories.
“A good fire is not as easy, though,” Glissen remarks. “I need more dry kindling!”

A campfire with supper and a few spooky stories later:
“Mmmm, I’m getting sleepy,” says Josephine, “How about you girls?”
“Yeah. I’m sleepy,” says Glissen.
“Sure! I’m getting tired, too,” says Primrose.
“Let’s go to-zzzz!” Diamond dozes off while talking.

Morning sunshine through the trees … and some strange noises wakes Glissen and Josephine.
“Do you hear that, Glissen?” Jo asks.
“Yes,” Glissen says sleepily. “Sounds like the day I interviewed at the animal shelter. Hush puppy, I’m trying to sleep!”
“I’m going to see what’s up,” Josephine says.

“Oh my! Look at these sweet puppies! Wake up, Glissen!”
“Uh, I am awake- there’s a puppy in my tent!”

The girls snuggle the little puppies.
“Primrose, come see! And Diamond…”

Diamond is still asleep.
“How she can sleep through this racket, I don’t know!” Primrose says.

Yip! Yip! The puppies are noisy in their affection. They love Glissen and Josephine!

After breakfast, the girls break camp.
“What should we do with the puppies?” Diamond asks.
“You mean Ollie and Coco?” says Glissen.
“Uh oh, if you’ve named them already-“
“They are DEFINITELY coming with us.” Glissen is adamant.

“Let’s take a photo of our epic camping trip!”
“Puppies and all!”
“But what will the others say about Ollie and Coco?”
“I’m sure it’ll be fine. It’s only 2 puppies!”

Chapter 2: The Hike

“I can’t believe they actually did it,” says Francesca, “You won’t catch ME sleeping in the woods!”
“I’m perfectly happy with the hike we’re going to take today,” Amy agrees.
“If we want to be back before dark, we”d better get going,” Cameron tells them.
“Ready!” Glossy and Sapphire chorus.

“These woods are beautiful!” Cameron says.
“Oh wow! It looks as though this path goes on FOREVER!” Sapphire exclaims, pointing to the trail in front of them.
“I agree. And I do love a well-marked path,” says Glossy.

The girls pause to admire a stream along the way. Sapphire stops to take a picture.
“It’s so pretty!”

“And refreshing for my hot feet!” Amy smiles.

The view from the top is amazing!

The girls head back down the trail, taking a shortcut through a small field.
But what’s that sound?
“It sounds like crying,” Francesca says.

“Here’s the path again- but look at that! Two wee puppies!” cries Cameron.
“Do you think they lost their mom?” Glossy wonders.

“I don’t know. They look pretty thin,” Francesca says.
“Oh my, they are so sweet, though!” Amy pats the little golden fur.

“And so friendly!” The little Spaniel sniffs Glossy’s hair. “I love him!”

“Aww, aren’t you a cutie! Who’s an adorable lil pup, huh?” Cameron coos as she cuddles the golden puppy.
Sapphire pets the puppy. “You have yourself a buddy, Cam,” she says. “I think these guys are coming home with us!”
“But what will the others say?” asks Francesca.
“Aw, it’s fine. Besides, it’s only 2 little puppies,” says Amy.

“It is a BIG house,” the others agree, “And there are only two…”
“Say cheese!” Sapphire cries.

“What are your plans for the day?” Glimmer asks the rest of the girls.

“And WE are headed to the lake!” chorus Lacey, Dazzle, Crystal and Grace.

At the Dazzle Dance Hall, Ruby tries out a new song. 🎵

“Oh my!” the girls exclaim. “He’s kind of cute for a little mutt,” says Em.
